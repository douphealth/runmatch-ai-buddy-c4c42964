How RunMatch AI works

Our deterministic scoring engine evaluates every shoe in a curated, manufacturer-verified database against your biomechanics and training profile. Each shoe is scored on cushioning level, heel-to-toe drop, stack height, midsole foam (PEBA, EVA, supercritical foam), carbon-plate presence, weight, support type (neutral, stability, motion control), and outsole grip. The result is a transparent match percentage, not a black-box LLM guess.
